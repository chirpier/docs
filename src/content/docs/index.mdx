# Overview

[Chirpier](https://www.chirpier.co) is the simplest way to monitor your data in real-time. Use Chirpier to capture data in your application - and visualize it immediately in real-time.

There are only two steps to visualising your data with Chirpier:
- First, send a simple event data packet to our service using one of our [SDK](/docs/emit/sdk) or [API](/docs/emit/api).
- Second, navigate to your [charts dashboard](https://www.chirpier.co/teams/charts), and create a [Chart](/docs/charts/charts). 

Start monitoring any of your data streams using just a few lines of code.
