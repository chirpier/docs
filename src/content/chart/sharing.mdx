# Sharing

## Share your chart URL

Once your chart is configured as shareable, you can:

- Copy the chart URL from the [Charts](https://www.chirpier.co/auth) page
- Share the password
- Anyone will be able to view the chart update in real-time as data is emitted to Chirpier
